Aligned with the UN Global Goals

YouthJobs SL is a Digital Public Good (DPG) directly supporting SDG Goal 8 — Decent Work and Economic Growth.

Sierra Leone has one of the highest youth unemployment rates in West Africa. By using digital technology to bridge the gap between employers and job-seekers, we create real economic opportunity for young Sierra Leoneans.

Our platform is free, accessible on mobile, and designed specifically for the Sierra Leone context — in partnership with local NGOs and businesses.
